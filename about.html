Our Story

About PearlSmile

Founded on a belief that exceptional dental care and genuine kindness should always go hand in hand.

Our Mission

Changing How People Feel About the Dentist

PearlSmile was founded in 2006 by Dr. Nadia Hassan, a prosthodontist who noticed that the most common barrier to good dental health wasn't access or cost — it was fear and a lack of trust in the dental experience itself.

She set out to build a practice that was different: one where patients felt genuinely heard, where the environment was calm and beautiful rather than clinical and intimidating, and where technology and skill were balanced by warmth and empathy.

Nearly two decades later, PearlSmile has treated over 12,000 patients, earned a consistent 4.9-star rating, and built a team of specialists who share that founding vision.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Everything we do flows from these principles.

🤝

Patient First

Every clinical decision and every aspect of our practice is built around the best interests of our patients — not convenience, not speed, not profit.

🔬

Clinical Excellence

We invest heavily in continued education, advanced technology, and evidence-based practice. Our team holds specialist qualifications from leading UK and international institutions.

💬

Honest Communication

We tell you exactly what we find, explain all your options, and never recommend treatment you don't need. You'll always leave knowing your full dental health picture.

🌱

Sustainability

We are committed to reducing our environmental impact — from paperless records and digital impressions to eco-friendly materials and responsible waste disposal.

♿

Accessibility

We believe everyone deserves excellent dental care. We offer payment plans, work with NHS patients in specific pathways, and provide anxiety management support.

🏆

Continuous Improvement

We seek feedback, measure outcomes, and constantly refine our processes. In 2024 we were recognised as one of the top 50 dental practices in the UK by Dental Excellence Magazine.
