Our Specialists

Meet the Team

A passionate, highly qualified group of dental professionals dedicated to your smile and wellbeing.

Dr. Nadia Hassan

Principal Dentist & Founder

With over 18 years of experience, Dr. Hassan founded PearlSmile with a vision to combine clinical excellence with genuine warmth. She specialises in full-mouth rehabilitation and complex restorative cases.

BDS (Hons) MClinDent Prosthodontics FDSRCS England

Dr. Oliver Park

Orthodontist

Dr. Park is our specialist orthodontist and a certified Invisalign® Diamond provider — placing him in the top 1% of providers globally. He sees patients of all ages, from early interceptive treatment to adult clear aligner therapy.

BDS MSc Orthodontics MOrth RCS Edinburgh

Dr. Priya Sharma

Cosmetic Dentist

Dr. Sharma has a keen eye for aesthetics and a true passion for smile transformations. She trained with leading cosmetic institutes in London and New York and regularly lectures on composite artistry and veneer techniques.

BDS PGCert Aesthetic Dentistry BACD Member

Dr. Marcus White

Implantologist & Oral Surgeon

Dr. White specialises in dental implants, surgical extractions, and bone grafting procedures. With over 3,000 implants placed, his precise surgical technique and calm chairside manner put even the most nervous patients at ease.

BDS (Glasgow) PGDip Implant Dentistry MFDS RCS

Dr. Sophie Chen

Paediatric Dentist

Dr. Chen is renowned for her extraordinary ability to connect with children, transforming even the most anxious young patients into confident dental visitors. She holds a specialist qualification in paediatric dentistry and has a dedicated gentle approach.

BDS MClinDent Paediatric Dentistry MFDS RCS England

Hannah Brooks

Lead Dental Hygienist

Hannah leads our hygiene team with enthusiasm and expertise. She is passionate about preventive care and patient education, empowering each patient with the knowledge to maintain excellent oral health at home.

DipDH BSc Oral Health Science GDC Registered
