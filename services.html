Preventive Care

General Dentistry

The foundation of a healthy mouth starts with regular preventive care. Our general dentistry services are designed to detect and treat issues early, saving you from more complex procedures down the line.

We use digital X-rays that emit up to 80% less radiation than traditional methods, allowing for precise diagnosis with minimal exposure.

  • Comprehensive oral health examinations
  • Professional teeth cleaning & scale and polish
  • Tooth-coloured composite fillings
  • Root canal treatment
  • Tooth extractions & wisdom teeth removal
  • Gum disease (periodontitis) treatment
  • Mouthguards for sport and bruxism

Straightening

Orthodontics

Straight teeth aren't just cosmetic — they're healthier and easier to clean. Our orthodontic options cater to all ages, from children's interceptive treatment to adult clear aligner therapy.

We're certified Invisalign® providers and offer digital treatment previews so you can see your results before you even start.

  • Invisalign® clear aligners
  • Traditional metal braces
  • Ceramic (tooth-coloured) braces
  • Lingual (hidden) braces
  • Retainers & post-treatment care
  • Interceptive orthodontics for children

Tooth Replacement

Dental Implants

Missing teeth affect more than your appearance — they impact your bite, speech, and bone density. Dental implants are the gold standard for permanent tooth replacement, functioning and looking just like natural teeth.

Our implant procedures use titanium posts that fuse with your jawbone over time, providing a stable foundation for custom-crafted crowns.

  • Single tooth implants
  • Implant-supported bridges
  • All-on-4 & All-on-6 full arch restoration
  • Mini implants for smaller spaces
  • Bone grafting if required
  • 3D cone-beam CT scanning for precise placement

Urgent Care

Emergency Dentistry

Dental emergencies don't keep business hours. Whether it's a sudden toothache at midnight or a knocked-out tooth during the weekend, we're here to help with priority same-day appointments.

Call our emergency line and a member of our team will advise you on immediate steps while we get you booked in as quickly as possible.

  • Severe toothache & abscess treatment
  • Chipped, broken, or knocked-out teeth
  • Lost fillings & crown re-cementation
  • Dental trauma from accidents
  • Soft tissue injuries to gums & lips
  • Swelling & infection management

Family Care

Pediatric Dentistry

Building positive dental habits early is one of the best gifts you can give your child. Our dedicated pediatric team creates a warm, fun, and anxiety-free environment that children actually look forward to visiting.

We use child-friendly language, gentle techniques, and a specially designed waiting area to make every visit a positive experience.

  • First dental visit from age 1
  • Fissure sealants to protect molars
  • Fluoride varnish applications
  • Children's fillings & extractions
  • Space maintainers for early tooth loss
  • Orthodontic assessment from age 7
